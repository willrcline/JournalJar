    Respond by text message twice a day: morning and night (plan and reflect). Rewire your brain and transform your life through this habit.

    FAQ

    How do I access my journal?

    Your Journal can be accessed any time from your phone by simply texting "-link" to your Benji phone number. Alternatively, you can find your journal link in the customer portal upon login.

    Is my journal private and secure?

    Journal documents are secured through Google Drive which involves 2 factor authentication and other security measures. Google is regarded as highly secure, and thus your journal is accessible soley through your own Google account.

    Can I customize my journal prompts?

    Benji Text Message Journal is highly configurable and can be customized in your customer portal upon login to alter from the default settings.
